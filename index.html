Admission Process:

Step 1- Apply Online — Visit the website and submit your application form.

Step 2- Entrance Test (RSAT / Scholarship Test) — Schedule your RSAT online on the portal.

Step 3- Interview — If shortlisted, schedule and attend your online interview.

Step 4- Offer Letter — Shortlisted students will receive the admission offer (typically within 3 working days after successful evaluation).

Step 5- Secure Seat — Confirm admission and complete payment/acceptance formalities within 15 days to secure your seat.

The Rishihood Scholastic Aptitude Test (RSAT) is designed to assess student’s aptitude, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. The test includes a video-based response and sections on Quantitative Aptitude and Psychometric Assessment.
